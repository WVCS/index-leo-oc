    INFO

    Located just off of the I-3000, Glove World is your one-stop shop for all things fun and amusement. Owned and operated by two brothers, Glove World started out as a small glove shop selling made-to-order gloves on the boardwalk of a small fishing town.

    It all started when they got a loan from a mysterious benefactor, unknowing of what gloves were and only interested in theme parks. They began work on the park, and it has since become the great Glove World that we all know today! The aforementioned benefactor has never been sighted since, but legends say you can hear their whispering around the park.
